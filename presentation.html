How It Works

From request to record in 4 steps

A simple, controlled flow that keeps everyone accountable.

1

User Submits

Staff fills out the request form with all logistics and payment details — Job ID, particulars, supplier, bank, and amount.

2

Admin Reviews

Request appears in the admin's Pending Approvals tab. Admin can approve with one click or reject with remarks.

3

Synced to Database

Approved requests are automatically written to the DATABASE sheet in Google Sheets for permanent record-keeping.

4

Audit Logged

Every step — who submitted, who approved, when — is logged in the AUDIT_LOG sheet with full traceability.
